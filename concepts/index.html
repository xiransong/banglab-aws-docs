  • Organization and Billing
    How BangLab uses an AWS Organization and where credits live

  • EC2 Basics
    What EC2 is, which region we use, and how vCPU quotas work.

  • S3 Basics
    What S3 is (and is not), how data is organized, and why it behaves differently from a filesystem.
